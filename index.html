How To Play

  • Select a Category: Choose from a variety of categories such as history, science, literature, or pop culture.

  • Choose a Point Value: Each category has questions of varying difficulty and point values. Pick a point value you feel confident about.

  • Read the Clue: Once you've chosen a category and point value, read the clue presented to you. Clues are given in the form of answers, and you must respond with the corresponding question.

  • Formulate Your Response: Respond to the clue by phrasing your answer in the form of a question. For example, if the clue is "This U.S. President served two non-consecutive terms," your response would be "Who is Grover Cleveland?"
